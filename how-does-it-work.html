How Does It Work

Zeveraars is a useful app designed specifically for KU Leuven students. Upon registering, you can upload your own avatar to represent yourself. After logging in, you can easily access your personal class schedule, ensuring you never miss a lesson again. The homepage is a 3D map of Campus Arenberg, which can be used to navigate to your classroom. By hovering over a building, you can view the name of the auditorium and the current class taking place. Clicking on the building will direct you to the appropriate class's chatroom where you can ask questions about the subject matter without disrupting the class. Additionally, users can start private conversations by selecting an avatar and clicking "chat". You can also add or remove friends to easily collaborate with other students. Overall, Zeveraars simplifies the life of a KU Leuven student.
