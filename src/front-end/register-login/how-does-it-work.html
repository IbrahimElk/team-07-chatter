How Does It Work

Our chat application, just like all the other chat applications, had a pretty basic working. It allows the users to send and receive text messages in real time over the internet. To use the chat application, you need to create an account. You can do this by clicking on the register page. If you already have an account, you can log in by clicking on the login page. Once logged in, the user can join channels, based on different courses , or chat with friends in private.

In a channel, your messages are visible to all participants in the channel. However, if you want to chat with a friend privately, you can do so by clicking on the user's name in your friendlist.

A message is sent simply by clicking the send button, ofcourse after you typed your message in the textfield. If you're a fast living person, you can also send your message by pressing the enter key.

After your session, it is convenient to log out. This can be done by clicking on the profile button in the right upper corner. Then you can click on the logout button. By doing this, you'll prevent that some other person can communicate with your fellow students and friends in your name. So be safe and log out!
