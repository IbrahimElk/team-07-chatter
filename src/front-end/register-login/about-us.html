About Us

We are eight students from the KU Leuven, working together to develop a chat application with a seamless front-end and robust functionality. Our team is comprised of three people from the Informatica program (Barteld, Vincent and Maite), and five people from the Engineering Science program (Ibrahim, Guust, Amelie, John and Thomas).

Our missions is to create a chap application that is both user-friendly and technologically advanced. Due to our backgrounds, we were able to complete this mission and present you our brand new chat application.

We hope you enjoy using our application as much as we enjoyed creating it!
