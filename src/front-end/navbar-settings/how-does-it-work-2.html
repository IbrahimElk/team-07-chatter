How Does It Work

Zeveraars is a handy app that is created especially for KU Leuven students. After registration, you can choose your own avatar, which you will use to represent yourself. Once you log in, you will be able to access your individual class schedule. This ensures that you will never miss a lesson again. The homepage, which is a 3D map of Campus Arenberg, can be used to find your way to the classroom. By hovering over one of the buildings, you can see the name of the auditorium and the class that is currently taking place there. If you click on the building, you'll be immediately directed to the appropriate class's chatroom. In this chatroom, you can ask any questions you have about the subject of this lesson. This makes it simple for you to communicate with the other students, without disrupting the class. Additionally, by selecting an avatar and then clicking “chat”, users have the opportunity to start private conversations. Finally, Zeveraars allows you to add or remove friends, making it simple to collaborate with other pupils. Overall, Zeveraars is a useful tool that makes your live as a student at KU Leuven much simpler.
