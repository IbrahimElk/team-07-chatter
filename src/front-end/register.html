About Us

A group of eight KU Leuven students created Zeveraars, a website designed specifically for KU Leuven students, to provide a user-friendly platform for communication, collaboration, and community-building, with a focus on ensuring privacy and security for its users.

How Does It Work

Zeveraars is an app designed for KU Leuven students, allowing them to access their class schedule, find classrooms on a 3D map of Campus Arenberg, join chatrooms, start private conversations, and collaborate with friends, making student life easier.
