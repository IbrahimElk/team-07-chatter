About Us

Welcome to Zeveraars, a website designed by 8 students from the KU Leuven, specifically for KU Leuven students. Our team is comprised of three people from the Informatica program (Barteld, Vincent and Maite), and five people from the Engineering Science program (Ibrahim, Guust, Amelie, John and Thomas).

Our goal is to make the lives of KU Leuven students easier through a platform where students can communicate with each other, even during class

Our website offers the abulity to ask questions and connect with fellow students. This can help you understand course material more easy and improve your grades. In addition, you can easily collaborate on projects and group assignments through our platform.

We belive that having a community of fellow students is essential for succesful studying and want to make this possible for all KU Leuven students. Our website is designed to be user-friendly, and we have worked hard to ensure the privacy and security for our users.

We hope you enjoy using our application as much as we enjoyed creating it!
